Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book your catering services?

We recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance for weddings and large events, and at least 2-4 weeks for smaller gatherings. However, we understand that sometimes events come together quickly, so please contact us even if your event is sooner - we'll do our best to accommodate you!

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions and allergies?

Absolutely! We take dietary restrictions and allergies very seriously. Our chefs are experienced in preparing meals for various dietary needs including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, dairy-free, and other specific allergies. Please inform us of any dietary requirements when you place your order.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve the Greater London area and surrounding counties. Delivery fees may apply for locations outside central London. For events beyond our standard service area, please contact us to discuss options - we're often able to accommodate special requests.

Do you provide staff for events?

Yes, we offer professional serving staff, bartenders, and event coordinators to ensure your event runs smoothly. Our staffing options can be tailored to your specific needs, from simple buffet service to full plated dinner service with professional waitstaff.
