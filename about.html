Who We Are

Gourmet Palette Catering was founded in 2005 by Executive Chef Michael Reynolds, who envisioned creating a catering company that would redefine event dining experiences. What started as a small operation with just two staff members has now grown into one of the region's most respected catering services.

Our philosophy is simple: exceptional food made with the freshest ingredients, impeccable service, and attention to every detail. We believe that food is more than just sustenanceâ€”it's an experience that brings people together and creates lasting memories.

Over the years, we've had the privilege of catering thousands of events, from intimate family gatherings to large corporate functions and lavish weddings. Each event is treated with the same level of care and dedication, ensuring that your special day is perfect in every way.
